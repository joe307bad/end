Void is the codename of a side project written by [Joe Badaczewski](https://joebad.com). At it's current stage, this project is a wargame where players vie for domination upon randomly generated planets of hexagonal territories. This is a multi-year case study in offline-first application development, highly performant browser-based software engineering, and building engaging digital literary fiction.
